Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

Orders & Shipping

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping typically takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days. Next-day delivery is available for orders placed before 2 PM EST. You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping times vary by location, typically 7-14 business days. Shipping costs and customs fees will be calculated at checkout.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also track your order by logging into your account and visiting the "My Orders" section.

Can I change my shipping address after ordering?

If your order hasn't been processed yet, contact our support team immediately to update your address. Once shipped, we cannot change the delivery address.

Payments & Billing

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers. All payments are securely processed.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely! We use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We never store your full credit card details on our servers.

When will I be charged for my order?

Your payment method will be charged immediately after you place your order. You'll receive a confirmation email once the transaction is complete.

Do you offer coupons or discount codes?

Yes! Sign up for our newsletter to receive exclusive discount codes and be the first to know about sales and promotions.

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for most items. Products must be unused, in original packaging, and in the same condition as received. Some restrictions apply to electronics and personal care items.

How do I initiate a return?

Log into your account, go to "My Orders," select the order, and click "Return Item." Follow the instructions to print a return label. You can also contact our support team for assistance.

How long does it take to get a refund?

Once we receive and inspect your return, refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be credited back to your original payment method.

Who pays for return shipping?

If the return is due to our error (wrong or defective item), we'll cover the return shipping costs. For other returns, the customer is responsible for return shipping fees.

Account & Security

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, fill in your details, and verify your email address. It's quick, free, and gives you access to order tracking, wishlists, and exclusive offers.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link. Follow the instructions to create a new password.

How do I update my account information?

Log into your account and go to "My Profile." You can update your name, email, password, shipping address, and other preferences there.

How do I delete my account?

Contact our support team to request account deletion. Please note that this action is permanent and cannot be undone.

Products

Are the products authentic?

Yes! We source all products directly from authorized distributors and manufacturers. Every item is 100% authentic and covered by manufacturer warranties.

Can I see product reviews before buying?

Absolutely! Each product page features customer reviews and ratings. You can also sort reviews by most helpful or most recent to make an informed decision.

What if an item is out of stock?

You can sign up for "Notify Me" on out-of-stock products. We'll email you as soon as the item is back in stock.

Do you offer product warranties?

Most products come with manufacturer warranties. Warranty details are listed on each product page. Extended warranty options are available for select items.

Still Have Questions?

Our support team is here to help. Get in touch with us and we'll respond within 24 hours.
